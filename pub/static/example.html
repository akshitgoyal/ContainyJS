Examples

Loved the idea so far? Lets look at some cool things this library can do!

Feature 1: Individual Cards

You can have individual cards on its own. Depending on the purpose, you can choose to include the picture or not.

Feature 2: Flip

If you click anywhere on the card, the card will flip to show its different view!
Woah! isn't that smooth?

Feature 3: Backview

Once flipped, you can see the backview! Here you can have detailed information about the cards.

Feature 4: About and Link Section

Once flipped, you can switch between About and Link section where About contains inforamtion about the card, and Link will have any addtional links related to the card!

Cool Fact: As an additional feature, when you click on the links or switch between about and link, it prevents the default behaviour of flipping!
What else? You don't need to worry about DOM, as it elegantly swithces without affecting other elements on your page!

Feature 5: Multiple Cards

You can have multiple cards in a big container. The DOM is adjusted automatically!

Feature 6: Expand and Collapse

If you feel you page is full of cards, you can collapse the cards in a small container and expand them whenever you want by clicking anywhere on the container!
Won't that mess my web page? Actually NO, the awesome thing about this library is, DOM is effeciently manipulated!
What else? Oh! in case you missed it, a collapse button was added so can can collapse the big container back!
