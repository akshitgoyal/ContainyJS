Containy.JS

Containy helps you make containers that can store cards. These can be used in multiple use cases like contact directories, Product pages and e-commerce websites like Amazon, photo walls, and many more..

Feature 1: You can have individual card on its own.

Feature 2: Click on the card to flip the card and reveal more information. Addtionally once the card is flipped, user can switch between sections in the card.

Feature 3: You can have multiple cards in a container.

Feature 4: You can expand/collapse the cards into a container.

Upcoming planned features before final submission(if time permits):

  • Adding a search bar to let users search in the containers
  • If the container reaches its capacity, then user can slide to next view to see other cards.
  • Allowing users to reposition the cards via drag and drop in the container (might not reach this feature as it can be very ambitious of me).
